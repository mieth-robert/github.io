[21/02/23] I gave a talk titled “Distributionally robust optimization for multiple data (re)sources” at the Energy Seminar Series at Johns Hopkins University hosted by Prof. Ben Hobbs.

[25/01/23] Our work on 100% electrified transportation in NYC was selected for an event at the 2023 Open Data Week.

[10/01/23] I presented my work (in collaboration with Prof. Juan Miguel Morales) “Physics- and privacy-aware data valuation in smart grids” at the Los Alamos Grid Science Winter School and conference.
