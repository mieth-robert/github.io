Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Princeton University. I received the Doctorate in engineering (Dr.-Ing.) degree from the Technical University of Berlin, Germany, in 2021. From 2018 to 2020, I was a Visiting Scholar and, from 2021 to 2022, a Postdoctoral Reseracher at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of New York University’s Tandon School of Engineering. My research interests include risk analysis, stochastic optimization, and data methods for modern power system operations and electricity markets.
