Robert Mieth

Postdoctoral Fellow

Princeton University

Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Princeton University. I received the Doctorate in engineering (Dr.-Ing.) degree from the Technical University of Berlin, Germany, in 2021. From 2018 to 2020, I was a Visiting Scholar and, from 2021 to 2022, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of New York University’s Tandon School of Engineering. My research interests include risk analysis, stochastic optimization, and data methods for modern power system operations and electricity markets.

Research topics include:

  • Power System Operation and Planning
  • Stochastic Optimization and Data Methods for Power Systems
  • Electricity Markets and Energy Economics
  • Decarbonization and Renewable Energy Integration
  • Power System Resiliencey and Cybersecurity
  • Distributed and Demand-Side Resources
